HOME ABOUT US


Ecovest Consultants Limited is Finance, Property and Business Consulting Company in Kenya.
The Firm was Registered in 2016 as a Business name later on converted to Private
Limited Liability Company in 2018. Ecovest Consultants Limited offers a range of Services which include: Audit, Accounting and Bookkeeping, Tax Services, Company & Business Registration Services And Property Marketing. We have clients in Nairobi, Kisumu Mombasa Eldoret and Nakuru. We have worked with Companies Such as, Norwegian Research
and Professional Institute, Greatlife Empowerment Kenya, Scantronic Africa Ltd, Dataways Technologies Limited, Center for Training and Development Kitengela and Many more. Through our team of Professional Associates, we strive to solve the Financial & Management Problems and model suitable solutions that are workable for them. We pride ourselves turning around dying Corporates
through our turnaround strategies and Management skills. Through Business leadership Acumen and an Experience of more than
4 years in the field, Ecovest Consultants Limited is an exceptionally reliable partner in matters Finance, Property and Business Consultancy.
Get in touch with Ecovest Consultants Limited for any of our Services.
