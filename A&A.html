Audit and Assurance:

In order to build solid foundations with effective management structures, businesses need to record their financial transactions, evaluate their systems and measure results. Our audit which covers assurance and advisory goes beyond compliance with statutory requirements. We adopt an added- value approach to the businesses of our clients, enhance productivity by listening to them, establish or make a model that considers all business implications.

Our aim is to ensure that clients meet their objectives, measure performance and manage risks adequately. We place strong emphasis on early planning and learning the unique aspects of a client’s business so as to have an effective, cost efficient, independent audit, performed in a timely manner.

The examples of audits we offer are as follows;

  • Statutory audit
  • Value for money audit
  • Project audit
  • Retirement and pension audit (RBAs)
  • Service charge audit
  • Provident
  • Cooperative societies (Sacco’s)
  • Trusts
