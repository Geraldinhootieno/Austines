The VAT(Electronic Tax Income) Regulations in kenya effective, Aug, 2022

HOME>The VAT(Electronic Tax Income) Regulations in kenya effective, Aug, 2022


On 25th, October, 2020, the Cabinet Secretary to Treasury signed in to law the regulation that requires businesses to integrate their systems with that of KRA to monitor the sales. The VAT (Electronic Tax Invoices) Regulations, 2020 was gazetted to ensure that there is complete sealing of the loopholes that have been experienced before while collecting the taxes. This is to ensure that every person registered under section 34 of the VAT act pays the correct amount of required tax.

A Tax Register is a an electronic invoicing and receipting system that is operated and maintained according to the regulations gazetted by the Cabinet secretary. The register must be availed, maintained and checked in accordance to the regulations.

The Register is a real time update of the sale by the taxpayer to the Authority. The invoice is first registered to KRA before the customer maybe pays for it.

Since the gazzettment of the regulation, the process is yet to take effect. This regulation since being published is going to be effective from August , 2022 and from this time going forth every taxpayer who is VAT registered shall be required to implement.

This is therefore an advice to all VAT registered individuals that you should begin jogging to implement the new developments in the tax field.












Ecovest Consultants Limited can assist you with the advisory service on the implementation of the tax register once it kicks off. It shall not be the ordinary ETR machine that we know but it shall be an integrated system approach which shall require the input of professionals.
